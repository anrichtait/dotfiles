
### 1. **Tahini Lemon Sauce**
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1/4 cup tahini
  - 2 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 clove garlic, minced
  - 1 tbsp maple syrup or agave nectar
  - 2-4 tbsp water (to thin)
  - Salt to taste

- **Instructions:**
  1. In a bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, minced garlic, and maple syrup.
  2. Gradually add water, one tablespoon at a time, until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Season with salt to taste.
  4. Use as a dressing, dip, or drizzle over roasted vegetables.

### 2. **Cashew Cream Sauce**
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1 cup raw cashews (soaked for at least 4 hours)
  - 1/2 cup water
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 clove garlic
  - Salt and pepper to taste

- **Instructions:**
  1. Drain and rinse the soaked cashews.
  2. Blend the cashews with water, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, and garlic until smooth.
  3. Add more water if needed to reach your preferred thickness.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. This creamy sauce works well over pasta, as a dip, or as a base for other flavors.

### 3. **Avocado Cilantro Sauce**
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  - 2 tbsp lime juice
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 1/4 cup water
  - Salt and pepper to taste

- **Instructions:**
  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Adjust the consistency with more water if needed.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Use this sauce for tacos, salads, or as a dip.
