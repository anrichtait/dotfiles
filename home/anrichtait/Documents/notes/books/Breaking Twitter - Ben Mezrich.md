## Summary
"Breaking Twitter" by Ben Mezrich provides an inside look at Elon Musk’s controversial acquisition of Twitter in 2022. The book details the events leading up to the takeover, the power struggles, and the impact of Musk’s management style on the company and its users. Mezrich draws on interviews, insider accounts, and his storytelling prowess to narrate the drama behind one of the most significant corporate takeovers in recent history.
