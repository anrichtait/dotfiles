## Summary
"The Antisocial Network" chronicles the extraordinary events surrounding the GameStop short squeeze in January 2021, where a group of amateur traders on Reddit’s WallStreetBets subreddit challenged Wall Street’s hedge funds. The book delves into how these retail investors turned the tables on traditional financial institutions by buying up GameStop stock, causing its price to soar and leading to significant losses for the hedge funds betting against it.
